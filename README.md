# 💫 About Me:
I'm a Full-Stack Developer & Project Manager based in Turku, Finland, working with clients across the Europe, USA, UAE, and the UK to turn IT problems into working, realistic software solutions.<br><br>Alongside my client work, I'm building Vitrix Technology a venture I founded to focus on the kind of product thinking I care about most: spotting where a market actually has a gap, and building practical software that solves it in real-life use, rather than over-engineering for its own sake. Through Vitrix, I'm working on multiple software solutions aimed at solving real, everyday problems for the people who'll actually use them.<br><br>My path here started in Surat, India, where I spent 7+ years working across the full stack from Senior Web Developer roles building ERP and business systems, to leading a development team of 15 at White Orange Software, to architecting multi-module systems as a Senior Software Developer at Delémont Studio. That stretch taught me two things at once: how to write production code that holds up, and how to lead the people writing it alongside me.<br><br>In 2023, I moved to Finland to complete a Bachelor's in Information and Communications Technology at Turku University of Applied Sciences, while continuing to work as a Fullstack Developer & Project Manager at theFIRMA managing client delivery end-to-end while staying hands-on in the codebase, a combination that's rare and one I've leaned into deliberately rather than splitting into two roles.<br><br>What ties the client and freelance work together is a straightforward approach: understanding the actual problem first, building software that solves it without unnecessary complexity, and staying involved through delivery rather than handing it off halfway. That holds true whether the role is a developer on the team or a project lead bridging business and engineering sides.<br><br>Vitrix Technology runs alongside that a separate venture applying the same instinct in a different direction: spotting real market gaps and building software to fill them, rather than solving one client's problem at a time.<br><br>What I bring to a team: full-stack capability, project ownership from day one, and a track record of delivering under real client pressure. That's the value I want to put to work next  for a Finnish or Nordic company, or for anyone with a problem worth solving.<br><br>📩 punitkathiriya@gmail.com
